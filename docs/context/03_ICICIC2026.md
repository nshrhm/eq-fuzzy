# ICICIC 2026 context

## Role

ICICIC 2026 is the **benchmark positioning / matched comparison** workstream inside the private `eq-fuzzy` monorepo.

The current ICICIC directories are placeholders only. Do not add fake benchmark adapters, configs, prompts, analysis code, or manuscript claims before the matched-comparison design is fixed.

## Main question

What does EQ-Fuzzy capture beyond existing emotion benchmarks?

## Scope

Use:

- matched comparison with selected existing emotion benchmarks
- aligned prompts, subsets, and scoring rules where feasible
- compact benchmark-methodology framing
- comparison matrices and coverage tables
- analysis of ambiguity, stability, controllability, persona sensitivity, or uncertainty-aware evaluation value

Do not use ICICIC to claim:

- a rerun of ICECCME human-grounded multilingual alignment
- full SCIS-style persona x temperature deconfounding
- annotation-workflow value for SPReAD1000

## Required separation from ICECCME

ICICIC should not center Japanese human-reference alignment ranking or EN/ZH drift plots. Those are ICECCME's main artifacts.

ICECCME results may provide background motivation, but the ICICIC contribution must be the matched comparison and positioning question.

## Required separation from SCIS

ICICIC should not center factorial main effects, interaction effects, or persona x temperature deconfounding. Those are SCIS's main artifacts.

## Future implementation anchors

- `configs/icicic/`

- `runs/icicic/`

Shared code should be extracted only when needed, with likely candidates in `src/iceccme2026/manifest.py`, `src/iceccme2026/metrics.py`, and `src/iceccme2026/model_scores.py`.

## Manuscript discipline

ICICIC can say:

- existing emotion benchmarks often emphasize accuracy, agreement, empathy, or classification-style performance
- EQ-Fuzzy can add uncertainty-aware and controllability-oriented signals
- the paper asks what EQ-Fuzzy adds, not whether EQ-Fuzzy replaces all existing benchmarks

ICICIC should not say:

- EQ-Fuzzy is universally superior to existing benchmarks
- this paper proves human validity by itself
- this paper resolves persona and temperature deconfounding
